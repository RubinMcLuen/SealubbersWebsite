What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember preferences, understand how visitors use pages, and deliver relevant advertising. We also use similar technologies such as localStorage for consent records.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary: Essential for the Site to function, such as load balancing or remembering your consent selections.
  • Performance & analytics: Help us understand traffic patterns through Cloudflare Web Analytics and improve content.
  • Advertising: Enable Google AdSense to deliver, measure, and personalize ads where permitted.

Typical Cookies & Purposes

Cookie / Storage Purpose Retention
cf_clearance / Cloudflare Maintains security and verifies genuine visitors. Up to 30 days
_cfuvid / Cloudflare Rate limiting and traffic management. Session
__gads / Google AdSense Measures ad interactions and prevents fraud. 13 months
IDE / Google Stores ad delivery and personalization choices. 13 months
sealubbers_cookie_consent (localStorage) Records whether you accepted or rejected cookies. Persisting until cleared
sealubbers_cookie_prefs (localStorage) Saves granular preferences for analytics, ads, and functional cookies. Persisting until cleared

Cookie names may change as we refine our stack. Advertising cookies are managed by Google; see Google Ads technologies and Google Analytics data practices for more detail.

How to Control Cookies

You can adjust cookie settings at any time:

  • Use the browser controls below to clear or block cookies: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings or the NAI opt-out.

Cookie Banner & Preferences

Our bottom banner informs you that we use cookies to improve your experience and serve ads. Selecting "Accept" stores consent in sealubbers_cookie_consent. Selecting "Manage" opens a light modal where you can toggle analytics, ads/personalization, and functional categories. These selections are stored in sealubbers_cookie_prefs and can be updated at any time via the "Manage" button.
