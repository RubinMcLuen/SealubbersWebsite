Scope & Definitions

This policy applies to sealubbers.com, related subdomains, and any content, forms, or interactive elements we host (the "Services"). "Personal data" means information that can identify you as an individual. By visiting or using the Services, you consent to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Usage and diagnostic data: Standard server logs, IP address, device details, pages viewed, and timestamps collected automatically by our hosting provider and Cloudflare Web Analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Small files stored on your device for remembering preferences, understanding traffic patterns, and serving relevant ads via Google AdSense. Details appear in our Cookie Policy.
  • Advertising identifiers: Google AdSense may collect pseudonymous identifiers and contextual data to deliver and personalize ads subject to your consent.
  • Contact form submissions: When you reach out, we collect the details you provide (such as name, email, and message content) to respond.

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and secure the Services.
  • Measure performance, diagnose issues, and understand what content resonates.
  • Serve contextual and personalized advertising through Google AdSense where permitted.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Comply with applicable law and enforce our Terms.

Legal Bases for EU Visitors

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process personal data based on legitimate interests (for example, securing and improving the Services) and, when required for analytics or advertising cookies, on your consent gathered through our cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time via the Manage option in the banner.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers: Google (AdSense) and Cloudflare supply analytics, advertising, and infrastructure support.
  • Compliance partners: We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, or protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers: If we explore a merger or acquisition, information may be reviewed under confidentiality.

Cookies & Tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies, analytics cookies (Cloudflare Web Analytics), and advertising cookies (Google AdSense). Please review our Cookie Policy for full details and browser-level controls. To learn how Google uses information from sites that partner with it, visit Google's partner sites page.

Data Retention

We retain server logs for up to 12 months unless longer retention is needed for security investigations. Contact form submissions are kept as long as necessary to respond and maintain relevant records. When data is no longer required, we delete or de-identify it.

Your Rights & Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, or object to certain processing, by emailing contact@sealubbers.com. To control personalized ads from Google, visit Google Ads Settings and the NAI opt-out portal. You can also manage cookie preferences through our on-site banner and your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child provided information, contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will post the revised version with a new effective date at the top of this page.
