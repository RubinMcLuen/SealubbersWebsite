The Crew of Krucia

A captain is only as strong as their crew. In Krucia, your shipmates are not just background figures. They are living characters who shape your journey. Each crewmate brings their own skills, quirks, and stories, making your crew one of the most important parts of the game.

Recruiting Your Crew

Crewmates can be found everywhere: in taverns, on the docks, wandering beaches, or rescued from shipwrecks. Some will join you for gold, others for favors or out of sheer curiosity. If you do not hire them right away, they remain part of the world. You might spot them again in another port, or even see them serving on rival ships.

Crew Traits and Gifts

Every crewmate is unique, defined by three qualities: Personality, Stats, and a Gift. Stats cover things like health, strength, sailing, and speed. Gifts are special traits tied to sailing, fighting, or exploration, ranging from faster cannon reloads to uncanny treasure sense. No two crewmates are alike, and finding the right mix is key to surviving the seas.

Crew Progression

  • Crew gain XP from battles and voyages.
  • Level ups increase stats and enhance gifts.
  • Higher levels may unlock personal quest steps or small bonuses.
  • Progress sticks with them. You can rehire former crew later.

Managing Your Crew

  • Crew cap is based on ship size. Smaller ships mean fewer crew. Larger ships mean more.
  • Hire, replace, and rehire to match your current goals.
  • Reward with gold or rum to keep morale high.
  • Dialogue-only orders for cosmetic tasks (for example, scrubbing the deck).
  • Dismiss politely, or make them walk the plank.

Crew Quests and Stories

Many crewmates have personal quests that reveal their past or ambitions. Completing these quests can unlock stronger gifts, unique rewards, or special opportunities. These stories give every crewmate a place in the larger world of Krucia.

Loyalty and Mutiny

Crew loyalty rises and falls with your success. Winning battles and rewarding your crew keeps spirits high. Repeated losses strain morale. If morale falls too low, mutiny becomes a real threat. A mutiny is a special event where your own crew turns against you, seeking your gold and your ship. To survive, you will have to fight back and reclaim command.

The Captain’s Legacy

Building your crew is one of the core loops of Krucia. From recruiting fresh faces in distant taverns to leveling up your most trusted shipmates, your crew defines your strength on the seas. Gold may buy ships, but it is the people aboard who will carry your story into legend.
