Beyond: Post-Release Roadmap

Beyond covers everything planned after full release. The goal is to keep Sealubbers growing with more activity in ports, new places to explore, and additional systems that expand the game without overcomplicating it.

Short Term

Ports: More NPCs, festival events, random occurrences, and new stories to make ports feel alive.

Exploration: New islands, biomes, and weather patterns will continue to expand the world.

Quest Packs: Multi-step stories and new treasure maps will be introduced.

Systems Additions

Two major updates are planned:

  • Sea Monsters: Encounters will include unique designs such as a hermit crab boss and a coral hive mind, alongside classic pirate creatures like kraken and sirens.
  • Fishing: A complex fishing system will be added, providing new ways to interact with the ocean.

Endgame and Replayability

New stars and achievements will be added with future content, creating ongoing goals for players. Seasonal challenges will also be introduced, offering special quests with unique rewards.

Balance, Quality of Life, and Longevity

Sealubbers will be updated regularly with balance adjustments, quality of life improvements, accessibility features, and performance upgrades.
