Frequently Asked Questions
When can I play Sealubbers?
I'm aiming to release the demo to the public by the end of September. I hope to release the full game sometime in 2026.
What platforms will Sealubbers support?
I want to release the game on PC, Mac, iOS, and Android. Possibly on console.
How much will Sealubbers cost?
The game will be free-to-play. There will be cosmetics available for purchase, but nothing that affects gameplay.
Is there multiplayer or co-op?
Not in the initial game. But I'm planning on making a multiplayer PvP gamemode inspired by games like Agar.io and Slither.io.
