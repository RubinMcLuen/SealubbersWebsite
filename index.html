Frequently Asked Questions
When can I play Sealubbers?
The demo is delayed beyond my original end-of-2025 goal. I'm focusing on finishing core features and polish first, then I'll share a date when I can confidently hit it.
What platforms will Sealubbers support?
I want to release the game on PC, Mac, iOS, and Android. Possibly on console.
How much will Sealubbers cost?
The game will be free-to-play. There will be cosmetics available for purchase, but nothing that affects gameplay.
Is there multiplayer or co-op?
Not in the initial demo. Right now I'm prioritizing single-player stability and content, then I'll revisit multiplayer plans afterward.
