Display Advertising

We work with Google AdSense to place display ads across the Site. Ads may be personalized using cookies or device identifiers where consent is given. Advertising revenue helps cover hosting, research, and ongoing improvements.

Affiliate Links

From time to time we include outbound links to products or services. If we participate in an affiliate program, we may earn a commission when you make a qualifying purchase. Prices are never increased for you, and we only link to items we believe are relevant.

Editorial Independence

Advertising and affiliate partners do not dictate our opinions or coverage. All editorial decisions are made by Plunder LLC. We do not accept payment for positive reviews, and sponsorships are clearly noted.

How We Label Paid Content

  • Display ads are served within clearly identifiable ad slots provided by Google.
  • Affiliate links appear within regular content and may include a short disclosure such as “This link may earn Sealubbers a commission.”
  • Sponsored posts, if any, will include a “Sponsored” or “Partner” label at the top of the article.
